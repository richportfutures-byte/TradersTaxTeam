# Non-Goals

LedgerFlow Personal MVP explicitly does **not** attempt to solve the following:

## Filing and compliance
- direct filing of federal, state, or Puerto Rico tax returns
- automated creation of final-ready Form 6781, Schedule C, or other filing outputs without review
- legal interpretation of residency, nexus, or Act 60 qualification
- determination of Trader Tax Status qualification

## Brokerage automation
- direct broker API ingestion
- live account syncing
- execution, order management, or performance journaling beyond imported accounting facts

## Enterprise/accounting breadth
- general ledger replacement for a business with multiple employees or entities
- multi-user permissions
- document management system for all receipts and tax records
- accountant collaboration portal in MVP

## Smartness theater
- no opaque "AI tax answer" surface that pretends certainty
- no silent override of imported data
- no hiding reconciliation failures behind dashboards

## Product posture
- not trying to be TurboTax
- not trying to be QuickBooks
- not trying to be a broker analytics suite

The MVP is intentionally narrow: trustworthy visibility into trading P&L, overhead, and estimated tax planning after reconciliation.
