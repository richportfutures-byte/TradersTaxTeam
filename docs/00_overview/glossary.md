# Glossary

## Gross P&L
Trading profit/loss before subtracting commissions and exchange/regulatory fees.

## Direct trade costs
Costs directly attached to execution, such as commissions and exchange/regulatory fees, usually already present in broker exports.

## Net P&L
Gross P&L minus direct trade costs.

## Overhead expenses
Business-use operating costs tracked separately from direct trade costs, such as platform software, market data, internet allocation, research subscriptions, professional fees, and office costs.

## Profit after costs
Net P&L minus overhead expenses.

## Reconciliation
The process of comparing internally computed totals against broker-provided statement totals to determine whether reporting is trustworthy.

## Reconciliation status
- GREEN: totals match within configured tolerance
- YELLOW: partial information, useful for provisional planning only
- RED: mismatch or integrity issue, do not trust reporting/export as final

## Rulepack
A versioned set of planning rules and assumptions used to compute estimated taxes, allocations, or validations. In MVP this may be implicit and simple, but the architecture keeps room for explicit versioned rulepacks.

## Set-aside target
The cash reserve amount the user should hold back for tax obligations based on configured rates and policy buffer.

## Trader Tax Status (TTS)
A tax status concept that may affect treatment of business expenses and activity characterization. This app does not determine whether the user qualifies.

## Act 60
Puerto Rico tax incentive regime that may matter to future planning, but is out of direct filing scope for MVP.
